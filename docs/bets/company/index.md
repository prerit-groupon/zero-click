# Company Bets

> Company Bets are large cross-functional projects sourced from STeam that we believe will
> meaningfully move our North Star goals. We call them "bets" because they require significant
> resources and have uncertain outcomes — just like placing a bet.
>
> Bets do not comprise all the work we do at Groupon. Our Bets have clear timelines and success
> criteria. We either prove they work and scale them up, or we stop them and try something else.

## Rules

- Only **3–8 Company Bets** active at a time

- Tracked in **Asana** for review and approval
- Run on clear **6–12 month timelines** with defined success criteria
