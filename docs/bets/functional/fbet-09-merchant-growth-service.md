# Functional Bet 9: Merchant Growth & Service Transformation

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Supply
> **Sponsor:** Barbara Weisz
> **DRI:** Robert Ruiz

---

## Goal

Transform Merchant Operations from a reactive ticket-handling function into a revenue-aligned
Supply Service that operates with clear accountability, measurable impact, and hard SLAs.

---

## Summary

This bet unifies every post-signature merchant interaction into a single, ranked queue system,
driving first-touch resolution and eliminating zero-value work. This allows:

- **Sellers** to focus on selling
- **Operations** to deliver top service levels to merchants, further retaining them

A new unit called **Merchant Growth** will focus on the critical **100 days** from signing a
contract through the first 90 days live — ensuring every merchant is set up and understands
how to be successful while working with Groupon.
