# Functional Bet 1: Marketplace Growth

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Marketing
> **Sponsor:** Josef Buryan
> **DRI:** Tomas Sikola

---

## Goal

Improve per-deal economics and portfolio quality while scaling volume, ensuring that growth
comes from stronger fundamentals rather than outlier performance.

---

## Summary

This bet strengthens marketplace performance by improving deal quality, allocation, and
visibility to drive sustainable growth across priority cities and categories. It was previously
a Company Bet and is now owned as a Functional Bet within Commercial to sharpen execution focus.

This includes improving:
- Merchant selection
- Deal structure
- Geographic allocation
- Category mix
