# Functional Bet 13: 1 Country, 1 Entity

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Finance
> **Sponsor:** Jiri Ponrt
> **DRI:** Pim Werdmolder

---

## Goal

Reduce structural complexity, cost leakage, and risk by aligning legal entities with where
value is actually created — starting with Track A (Ireland/IP) in 2025–Q1 2026.

---

## Summary

This bet simplifies Groupon's legal and financial structure by moving away from a legacy,
highly complex entity model that no longer reflects how the business operates today.

The focus is on:
- Rationalising the entity structure country by country
- Eliminating unnecessary legal overhead and compliance cost
- Aligning tax and IP structures with current business reality
