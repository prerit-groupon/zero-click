# Functional Bet 15: Payment on Fulfillment Ingresso — INTL

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Supply
> **Sponsor:** Peter Sulak
> **DRI:** Quentin Fodda

---

## Goal

Enable fulfilment-based payments internationally so Groupon can support ticket formats such
as open-dated, multi-day, and period passes while meeting accounting, tax, and reconciliation
requirements across markets.

---

## Summary

This bet unlocks international growth in Tours, Tickets & Activities by enabling Payment on
Fulfilment (POF) for Ingresso integrations outside North America. Today, international
connectivity is constrained by Pay-On-Redemption–only support, which blocks key ticket types
and prevents major partners from launching.

Key workstreams:
- Extend POF support to international markets
- Support open-dated, multi-day, and period pass ticket formats
- Meet accounting, tax, and reconciliation requirements per market
- Unblock major international partners from launching on Groupon
