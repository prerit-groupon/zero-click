# Functional Bet 5: Merchant Growth AI Deal Generator (AI DG)

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Product
> **Sponsor:** Masha Sharma
> **DRI:** Lenka Bartonickova

---

## Long-Term Goal

Make deal creation faster, smarter, and more profitable — reducing manual time by a third
while improving the quality and conversion potential of every deal.

## Short-Term Goal

Improve sales rep productivity by simplifying and speeding up the deal-making process in
the initial phase, enabling sales reps to close more deals.

---

## Summary

This bet transforms how deals are created and optimized on Groupon by using AI to:

- Simplify deal creation workflows
- Score deals for quality and conversion potential
- Accelerate merchant onboarding
