# Functional Bet 11: GRPN Account Security — Bot + ATO

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Finance
> **Sponsor:** Jiri Ponrt
> **DRI:** Tomasz Aniśko

---

## Goal

Build a layered, friction-aware security system that stops automated abuse without introducing
unnecessary barriers for legitimate customers.

---

## Summary

This bet strengthens Groupon's defenses against bot-driven fraud and account takeovers (ATO)
while preserving conversion and customer trust. Guided by GROW², the team is:

- Deploying full bot-protection coverage across legacy and MBNXT platforms
- Improving login and checkout telemetry
- Rolling out modern authentication tools (OTP and social logins)

---

## Results

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Incremental recovered revenue | $2.7M |
| Additional annual orders (false-decline recovery) | 160K |
| Chargeback losses | Stable within 15–25 bps of GMV |

Device-management features and OTP safeguards will ensure account integrity while maintaining
or improving conversion rates.

---

## Outcome

A secure, data-driven commerce platform that balances protection and performance — eliminating
fraudulent behavior, reducing financial exposure, and giving legitimate customers a faster,
safer checkout experience.
