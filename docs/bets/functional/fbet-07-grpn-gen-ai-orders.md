# Functional Bet 7: GRPN Gen. AI — Allowing Orders with AI Agents

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Engineering
> **Sponsor:** Dusan Senkypl
> **DRI:** Roland Abou Younes

---

## Goal

Design a secure, low-latency API layer that turns Groupon into an AI-native marketplace,
where agents can transact with the same reliability and safeguards as our own platform.

---

## Summary

This bet makes Groupon bookable with AI agents — building the infrastructure that allows
trusted partners like OpenAI and Google to safely discover, price, and place Groupon orders
through conversational interfaces. Guided by GROW², the team is creating:

- A unified Search and MCP (Merchant Control Platform) system
- Standardized data, pricing, and checkout
- Policy controls enforced for every AI agent interaction

---

## Results

- Consistent growth from AI-driven traffic and new partner integrations
- Improved catalog accessibility and checkout performance

---

## Outcome

A future-proof Groupon — one where intelligent agents can connect users to local experiences
directly, expanding reach, driving incremental value, and positioning Groupon at the forefront
of AI-enabled commerce.
