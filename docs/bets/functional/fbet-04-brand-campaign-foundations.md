# Functional Bet 4: Brand Campaign Foundations for 2026

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Marketing
> **Sponsor:** Matías Coumet
> **DRI:** Eduard Martínez Arcediano

---

## Goal

Understand how brand activity drives incremental demand, customer acquisition, and downstream
performance beyond last-click attribution — to inform a scalable Brand Strategy for 2026.

---

## Summary

This bet tested and validated the role of brand investment as a demand-generation engine.
Guided by the GROW² framework, the team:

- Ran a multi-market brand campaign across flagship and spark cities
- Measured impact using brand lift, search lift, direct traffic, and customer growth
- Evaluated channel roles and attribution limits
- Identified where brand spend delivers the highest incremental return

---

## Outcome

A validated, data-backed Brand Strategy for 2026 that clearly links brand investment to
incremental demand and customer growth.
