# Functional Bet 2: Reviews as a Trust & Growth Engine

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Product
> **Sponsor:** Marie Havlíčková
> **DRI:** Jason Wong

---

## Goal

Transform customer feedback — through easier review submission, smarter follow-up questions,
introducing NPS, and AI-powered review highlights — into a trust engine that fuels Groupon's growth.

---

## Three Focus Areas (GROW²)

1. **Capture more reviews** — make submission effortless across all touchpoints
2. **Make reviews richer and more trustworthy** — smarter follow-up, NPS, AI highlights
3. **Improve how customers discover and use them** — better deal conversion through social proof

---

## Results

- Faster, richer, and more consistent feedback
- Higher participation rates
- More authentic content
- Better deal conversion

---

## Outcome

A more trusted Groupon marketplace where real experiences build reputation, loyalty, and
long-term growth.
