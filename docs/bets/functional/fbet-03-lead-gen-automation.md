# Functional Bet 3: Lead Gen & Automation Engine

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Supply (Commercial)
> **Sponsor:** Filip Popovic
> **DRI:** Chris Hill

---

## Goal

Create a reliable engine that produces high-quality, accurately scored leads that sales teams
trust — and can turn into real, profitable growth.

---

## Summary

This bet reimagines how Groupon finds and converts new merchants by turning manual lead
generation into an automated, data-driven system. Using the GROW² framework, the team is
building a smarter pipeline that blends AI, automation, and predictive modeling to:

- Identify the best prospects
- Route them quickly
- Measure conversion in real time

---

## Results

- Faster follow-up
- Higher-quality meetings
- Stronger sales adoption
- Integrated workflows between Clay, Salesforce, and outbound campaigns

---

## Outcome

A scalable, intelligent lead system that helps every rep focus on what matters most —
building relationships, closing deals, and driving consistent growth.
