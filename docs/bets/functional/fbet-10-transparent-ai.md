# Functional Bet 10: Transparent AI in Groupon

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Engineering
> **Sponsor:** Ales Drabek
> **DRI:** Martin Majer

---

## Goal

Give every employee instant, secure access to Large Language Models through a centralized
Groupon AI Hub — no setup, no approvals, just log in and start working smarter.

---

## Summary

This bet accelerates company-wide AI adoption by removing friction, reducing waste, and
making AI usage measurable and continuously improving. Using GROW², Engineering is building:

- An **Okta-connected, SCIM-managed LLM Gateway** that tracks spend and controls access
- **Weekly automated feedback** on how teammates can improve their prompts and productivity
- Full governance: every model, key, and dollar spent on AI tracked and optimized

---

## Results

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Employees onboarded | 100% with zero setup time |
| Spend tracked | 95% of all AI spend governed |
| Prompt quality improvement | +40% within 3 months |
| NPS | Above +30 by early 2026 |
| Non-technical productivity lift | ~5% |
| Annual savings | ~$4.3M |

---

## Outcome

A transparent, accountable, and performance-driven AI ecosystem that raises non-technical
employee productivity while ensuring every model, key, and dollar spent on AI is optimized
for measurable business impact.
