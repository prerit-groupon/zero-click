# Functional Bet 8: Customer AI Support

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** GO (Groupon Operations)
> **Sponsor:** Petr Bulka
> **DRI:** Kveta Vostra

---

## Goal

Deliver instant, effortless resolutions through automation, smart routing, and AI-driven
assistance that makes every interaction faster, simpler, and more human.

---

## Summary

This bet transforms Groupon's customer support from a cost center into a loyalty engine.
Using GROW², the team is building a unified platform — powered by Groupon Nodegraph — that
connects customers, merchants, and agents in one place to ensure every issue is resolved
consistently and with empathy.

Key workstreams:
- Deflect 50% of current human-handled contacts while maintaining satisfaction scores
- Introduce proactive engagement for select high-value deals
- Condense hundreds of manual agent workflows into a smaller, highly skilled team focused on helping AI make better decisions

---

## Results

- Higher customer satisfaction (CSAT)
- Lower contact rates
- Improved post-support repurchase

---

## Outcome

A world-class support experience that becomes a true differentiator for Groupon — where
fast, reliable help builds trust, repeat use, and brand advocacy.
