# Functional Bet 6: Location as an Enabler

> **Type:** Functional Bet
> **Org:** Engineering
> **Sponsor:** Dusan Senkypl
> **DRI:** Jakub Dado

---

## Goal

Determine each customer's location accurately and consistently — whether at home, at work,
or on the go — and use that data to power personalization in Search, Relevance, and
Managed Channels.

---

## Summary

This bet builds the foundation for smarter, more personalized experiences across Groupon
by creating a single, reliable Location Service. Guided by GROW², the team is:

- Unifying how location is captured and shared across platforms
- Fixing inconsistencies between app and web
- Improving edge-case handling in metro areas where divisions overlap
- Reworking the location selector UX to better reflect user intent (traveling, gifting, browsing locally)

---

## Results

- More accurate targeting
- Measurable lift in conversion and click-through rates
- Customers see deals that are truly relevant to where they are

---

## Outcome

A platform-wide single source of truth for location — enabling more precise, ML-driven
personalization and laying the groundwork for every team that depends on knowing where
our customers are.
